About this project

Kozha is made by “minimal concept”. This project expects to make a cosmetic e-commerce website. I want users scrolling down the website by designing the primary button in a specific area.

I only use 3 colors for this website and divide product categories to guide users into the sale pages. It looks simple but I pay attention to a lot of detail.

Play with space

I would like to play with space. The primary button was arranged into the bottom page because I want the user to try to scroll down and make time to look around on the website.

Photo slide menu

I put a little trick to the photo slide menu to smooth the user’s feeling and make the attention to the highlight photo product.

Flowchart

Base on the flowchart. The whole process is the standard for an e-commerce website. Keep clean and clear for the user. I split the products into 4 parts and use the categories to divine them. I show the picture of the product in a 100% proportion.
