User journey map

Make user journey map for check and helping me understand the users.

1.Sign in / Sign up

A. Sign up for enter to the Application.

B. Having Facebook option to sign up.

2.BMI Calculator

A. Choose Activity and Purpose of exercise.

B. Put your Age/ Weight / Height.

C. Create personal course.

3.Take to main page

A. Start your Workout..

B. See the list of Exercise.

4.Choose program

A. Start your Workout program.

B. Application will calculate the exercise program.

C. Workout and Exercise according to the list provided.

5.Body Statistic

A. Show your calrories burned.

B. See improvement in your exercise.

I made a user journey map after finished the user persona. I sorted the action of this application, define the users much or want to do, observe the user’s feelings, and try to improve the flow of the application.

I got a lot of the user’s obstacles from the user journey map. Such as I noticed that Users who have never exercised will be concerned and unfamiliar with specific workout words or confused with the body statistic.

Ux research

Persona

A user persona is used in this project because I want to put myself in the user’s shoes and highlight their pain points. I especially care about fitness beginners due to this is one of the purposes of this application to make people want to exercise.

I created a user persona from a group of people who have ever and have never exercised online. It helps me expand my aspect of this service.
